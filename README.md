📊 Project Title / Headline
HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition Insights

📌 Short Description / Purpose
This dashboard analyzes employee data to identify key factors driving attrition. It helps HR teams make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

⚙️ Tech Stack
Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
Excel / CSV (Data Source & Cleaning)
DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)
📂 Data Source
HR dataset (CSV file) containing employee details such as age, salary, job role, education, attrition, etc.
Data includes 1470 employee records.
🚀 Features / Highlights
🔴 Business Problem

High employee attrition leads to increased hiring costs, reduced productivity, and loss of experienced talent. The organization needs insights into why employees are leaving.

🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
To deliver an interactive visual tool that:-

Tracks overall attrition rate
Identifies high-risk employee segments
Analyzes attrition based on salary, age, education, and job role
Supports HR in making strategic retention decisions

📈 Business Impact & Insights
Salary Impact:-
Employees with lower salaries (≤5K) are more likely to leave → need salary restructuring.
Age Factor:
Employees aged 26–35 have the highest attrition → focus on mid-career engagement.
Job Role Risk:
Roles like Sales Executive & Lab Technician show high turnover → role-specific retention strategies needed.
Department Insight:
Attrition varies across departments → targeted HR policies required.

Workforce Strategy:
Helps HR teams:
Reduce hiring costs
Improve employee satisfaction
Plan retention strategies effectively
